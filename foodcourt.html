FOODCOURT

Discover the Food Court: Where Flavor Takes Center Stage Welcome to the heart of culinary delights at our Food Court! Whether you're craving international cuisine, comfort food classics, or healthy options, our diverse selection of eateries promises to satisfy every palate. From quick bites to leisurely meals, the Food Court offers a vibrant dining experience for shoppers of all ages.

Special Offerings:

International Flavors: Indulge in mouthwatering dishes from around the world, from sushi to tacos. Family-Friendly Choices: Enjoy kid-friendly meals and hearty options for the whole family. Healthy Alternatives: Choose from fresh salads, smoothies, and wholesome snacks for a nutritious boost.

Find Us in the Mall:

The Food Court is centrally located on the second floor of the Grand Plaza Mall. Here’s how to find us:

Enter the mall through the main entrance. Take the central escalator or elevator to the second floor. Follow the signs towards the Food Court, located near the indoor playground and cinema complex.

Whether you're refueling between shopping sprees or gathering with friends, the Food Court is your go-to destination for delicious dining experiences. Bon appétit!
